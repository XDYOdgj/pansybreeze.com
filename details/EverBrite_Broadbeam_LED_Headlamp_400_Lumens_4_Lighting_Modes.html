EverBrite Broadbeam LED Headlamp

Stay illuminated and confident on every outdoor journey with the EverBrite Broadbeam LED Headlamp. Whether you're exploring the wilderness, camped under the stars, or working after dark, this headlamp delivers the perfect blend of power, portability, and durability.

Unmatched Brightness & Visibility

The EverBrite Broadbeam LED Headlamp boasts a 210-degree wide-angle illumination, casting light across a broad area to keep you fully visible in even the darkest conditions. With a 400 lumen output, it can illuminate your surroundings up to 95 feet away, making it an essential tool for any nighttime activity.

Versatile Lighting Modes

Designed for flexibility, this headlamp features 4 lighting modes so you can choose the right setting for your task. From a wide flood light to a focused spotlight, it adapts to your needs with a simple zoomable focus control. Simply push in or out the front of the light to adjust the beam pattern effortlessly.

Built for Long-Term Use

Durability meets comfort with the EverBrite Broadbeam LED Headlamp. It features a robust aluminum casing that resists wear and tear, while its lightweight design ensures it’s easy to wear for hours. Get up to 5 hours of runtime on a single charge so you never need to stop for light.

Secure & Comfortable Fit

The bendable and stretchable headband offers a customizable fit, keeping the headlamp securely in place during intense activity. It’s designed for all-day use and ensures you stay focused on your goals without worrying about the light slipping or straining your head.
