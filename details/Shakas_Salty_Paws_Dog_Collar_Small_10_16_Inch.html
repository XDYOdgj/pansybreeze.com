Shaka's Salty Paws Dog Collar

A stylish and functional dog collar that adds personality to your pet's look while being kind to the planet. Perfect for small dogs up to 25 lbs, this collar combines vibrant designs with eco-conscious materials to deliver both durability and comfort.

Vibrant & Durable Design

Inspired by the spirit of surfing, snowboarding, and music culture, Shaka's Salty Paws Dog Collar features bold, fade-resistant prints. These designs are not only eye-catching but also built to withstand the rigors of outdoor activities and daily adventures.

Built for Comfort

Crafted from soft, flexible webbing with no harsh edges, this collar ensures your dog stays comfortable all day long. The gentle material prevents irritation and is perfect for those with sensitive skin, making it ideal for everyday wear.

Adjustable Fit & Easy Use

Designed with adjustable sizing that fits dogs from 10 to 16 inches around, the collar offers a secure and tailored fit. Its pre-curved quick release buckle allows for one-handed on-and-off convenience, making it easy to use during walks or playtime.

Eco-Conscious Materials

Shaka's Salty Paws Dog Collar is made from recycled materials, repurposing plastic bottles into high-quality, sustainable dog gear. This not only reduces environmental impact but also ensures the same strength and reliability as traditional options, offering peace of mind with every use.

Long-Lasting Durability

With a lifetime warranty on materials and workmanship, this collar is built to last. The high tensile strength webbing ensures it maintains its shape and integrity over time, making it a reliable choice for pet parents who want long-term value.

Easy to Clean & Maintain

The design is easy to clean, making it simple to maintain for daily use. Whether you're heading to the beach, the mountain, or just about town, this collar stays fresh and functional with minimal effort.

From beach days to daily walks, Shaka's Salty Paws Dog Collar is the perfect blend of style, comfort, and sustainability for your furry friend.
