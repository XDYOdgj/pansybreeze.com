Intex Dura-Beam Air Mattress: Perfect for Camping and Guest Beds

The Intex Dura-Beam Air Mattress is designed to deliver comfort and support for any sleeping situation. Made with 93% PVC, it offers a balance of durability and softness, ensuring long-lasting use without compromising on feel. This unique air mattress features Fiber-Tech Beam Construction, which includes thousands of high-strength polyester fibers to provide stability and a supportive sleep surface, ideal for a restful night away from home.

With a 10-inch height, it keeps your feet elevated and promotes better air circulation, making it especially great for camping. The 2-in-1 valve offers easy inflation and deflation with an extra wide opening, saving you time and effort. Its 600lb weight capacity ensures it can support most users, offering a sturdy base for comfort and confidence. The velvety sleeping surface is soft and easy to clean, perfect for both outdoor adventures and indoor guest beds.
