The Umbrauto Truck Bed Air Mattress

Experience the perfect blend of comfort and rugged reliability with the Umbrauto Truck Bed Air Mattress. Built to fit 6-6.5ft full size truck beds, it's designed to provide a safe and spacious sleeping surface for 2-3 people, regardless of where your adventure takes you.

Why You'll Love It

Crafted with a plush soft-flocked top, this mattress delivers a cozy, cushioned feel to ensure restful sleep in the great outdoors. Meanwhile, the durable PVC and reinforced 210T grid fabric on the underside offer superior resistance against punctures and air leaks, so you can focus on the journey, not the worry.

Built for Adventure

Whether you're hitting the trail solo or embarking on a family road trip, this inflatable mattress is a must-have. It's ideal for truck tent camping, backyard getaways, or any situation where you need a portable, reliable sleep solution. The internal wave straps provide sturdy support, up to 260 kg (600 lbs), making it perfect for long drives and rugged outdoor conditions.

Easy Setup & Durability

Included with the mattress is an electric pump and a 3-meter cable, making inflation and deflation quick and hassle-free. Plus, with built-in repair patches and a compact storage bag, you'll have everything you need to keep it in excellent condition for every trip.

For All Your Outdoor Needs

This outdoor sleeping mattress is engineered for performance, offering both comfort and durability without compromising on portability. It's the ultimate solution for anyone who loves camping and needs a dependable air mattress with pump for their vehicle's bed.
