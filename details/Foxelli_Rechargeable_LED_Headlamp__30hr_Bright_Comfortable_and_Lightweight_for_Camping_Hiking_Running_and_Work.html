Foxelli Rechargeable LED Headlamp

Light up your next adventure with the Foxelli Rechargeable LED Headlamp. This ultra-bright and versatile headlamp is designed to keep you safe and comfortable in any outdoor setting. Featuring a powerful 160-lumen LED light, it provides up to 200 feet of visibility, making it the perfect choice for late-night hikes, camping, and even running under low light conditions.

With a 4-hour charge offering up to 30 hours of use, this headlamp is ideal for long treks and extended activities. The 60-degree tilt feature allows you to direct the light exactly where you need it, whether you're navigating trails or working in the dark.

It's designed for both adults and kids, with 5 light modes and white/red color options to suit your needs. Whether you're on a family trip or an outdoor expedition, this headlamp is the perfect choice for bright, reliable light that keeps up with your lifestyle.
